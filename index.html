Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🔐
Privacy First

All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM via Apple's CryptoKit and stored exclusively on your Mac. No cloud. No accounts. No network access.

✨
Automatic Capture

Runs silently in the background using macOS Accessibility API. Captures across every app—Notes, Mail, Slack, browsers, IDEs, and more.

⚡
Instant Search

Full-text search across your entire history. Find that email draft you closed, that form you lost, that message you accidentally deleted.

🪶
Featherlight

Under 1 MB RAM. Near-zero CPU. Native SwiftUI + AppKit—no Electron, no web views. Built to disappear completely into the background.

How It Works

Up and running
in 60 seconds.

01

Install & Allow

Download the app and grant Accessibility permissions in System Settings. One-time setup, takes about 30 seconds.

02

Type Normally

Use your Mac exactly as you always do. TypeSave runs invisibly in the background—zero interruption to your workflow.

03

Recover Instantly

Lost something? Click the menu bar icon, search your history, copy your text back. Done in seconds—not frustration.

Privacy

Your words.
Your machine.
Full stop.

🔒
AES-256-GCM Encryption
Every capture is encrypted via Apple's CryptoKit before being written to disk. The key never leaves your device—not even to us.
🚫
Zero Network Access
No analytics, no telemetry, no cloud sync. TypeSave holds no network entitlements—verify it yourself in Little Snitch.
🗑️
Auto-Delete After 30 Days
All data is permanently deleted after 30 days by default. Fully configurable. Your typing history doesn't outlast its usefulness.
📂
Open Source & Auditable
Every line of source code is public on GitHub under the MIT License. Audit it, fork it, build it yourself. No black boxes.
🛡️
Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We can't read your data. Not because of policy—because of math.
